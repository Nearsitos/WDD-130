Frequently Asked Questions

Custom PC's

What customization options do you offer?

We offer a wide range of customization options including CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, cooling solutions, and more. Please visit our customization page for detailed information.

Absolutely! We understand that everyone has unique needs and preferences. Feel free to contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements, and we'll be happy to assist you in creating your dream PC.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal for secure online payments.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer financing options through our trusted partners. Please visit our financing page for more information.

Shipping

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.

Warranty

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a standard warranty on all our products, covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions. Please refer to our warranty page for detailed terms and conditions.

How do I make a warranty claim?

If you encounter any issues with your product during the warranty period, please contact our customer support team. They will guide you through the process of making a warranty claim.
